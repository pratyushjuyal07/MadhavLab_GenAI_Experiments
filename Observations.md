## Observations

### Initial observations with base model
- Detailed prompts improved coherence, atmosphere and instrument harmony
- Specific instructions (e.g. "light tabla beats") influenced instrument tone and significantly changed audio style
- Longer generations introduced noise and decreased synchronicity of beats and melody
- Instrument consistency varied across prompts, but detailed prompts greatly improved quality
- Broad global genres (e.g. electronic rock) produced better outputs than Indian classical prompts, if provided with the same prompt quality
