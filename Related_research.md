# Research Review: Fine-Tuning MusicGen for Indian Classical Music
RagaGEN: Fine-Tuning MusicGen Transformer Models for the Generation of Indian Classical Raga Melodies  
- D Sreekanth, S Gopi, F Ojo - 2025

## Key Observations

* Demonstrates feasibility of adapting a general text-to-music model to a specific Indian classical style.
* No clear description of trainable parameters, training configuration, or computational requirements.
* Relies heavily on preprocessing and post-processing rather than architectural innovations.
* Uses a limited dataset focused on a single raga.
* Evaluation is primarily based on note distributions and signal analysis rather than human listening studies.

## Future Directions

* Multi-raga training and evaluation.
* LoRA-based parameter-efficient fine-tuning.
* Larger instrumental datasets.
* Human evaluation by trained classical musicians.
* Stronger benchmarking against baseline MusicGen models.
